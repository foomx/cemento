Ardex

Smoothing and leveling compounds that ensure a smooth, flat and level base are essential for a successful wall or floor installation. ARDEX manufactures a range of smoothing compounds and self-levelers for internal and external use that are the industry benchmark for contractors, architects, and installers

Thanks to their low shrinkage, they do not slump and are ideal for smoothing and floating floor, wall and ceiling areas. There’s no need for tiresome re-smoothing because the products cure without cracking – in all coat thicknesses. Rapid setting and quick drying properties ensure a floor can be walked on in as little as 1 hour ensuring overnight and even same day turnarounds.
