About

Cemento Sdn Bhd is a cementitious finishing artisan in Malaysia, working with different cementitious products around the world that give unique, attractive, quality finishing to our clients and consultants.

We have products ranges from indoor to outdoor floors, walls, and ceilings that required minimal joints, producing seamless and continuous top up finishing which eliminates gaps that often trap dirt and germs overtime.

We care about quality product delivery. Our team have years of experiences and we continuously innovate and improve the quality of our products.
