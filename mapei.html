Mapei

SL floor is a 5mm thickness natural warm grey colour self-leveling cementitious floor system. It is a floor system that can be apply directly on to existing tiles, concrete, marble, and other solid surfaces. No hacking on existing tiles will be needed, perfect for refurbishment your space without creating more construction landfill. Application indoor areas only.
